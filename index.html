Kentucky Hoops Source

Kentucky Hoops Source is a website I made to allow for easier viewing of Kentucky High School Basketball Stats. You can think of it as a basketball reference for high school basketball in Kentucky. Personally I find it to be the easiest way to view stats as other sources don't allow you to see the stats of both teams on the same page or have a way of viewing aggregated stats such as career or season-long stats

KHS Features

View Game Stats for both teams and participate in live discussion

View Season / Career stats for players. Add pictures of your favorite players

View team rosters and stats quickly!
